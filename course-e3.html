Course Details

This 2-hour Fire Safety for Early Years online course supports staff in early years settings to understand fire safety responsibilities, carry out risk assessments and implement safe evacuation procedures. It is designed to meet the needs of nurseries, preschools and childminders.

Who should take this course

Ideal for nursery practitioners, preschool staff, childminders and early years teachers with responsibility for children and safety in early years environments. No prior training required.

Assessment and Certification

The course uses interactive quizzes, videos and self-checks. A multiple-choice assessment at the end requires a 70% pass mark. Successful learners receive a downloadable CPD-accredited certificate and 2 CPD points. This course is approved by FAIB.

Course Content

  • The importance of fire safety in Early Years settings
  • Key fire safety regulations and legal requirements
  • Conducting effective fire risk assessments in early years environments
  • Common causes of fire and managing heat and ignition sources
  • Understanding the fire triangle and how fire spreads
  • Safe use and management of electrical equipment
  • Fire prevention and control measures tailored to early years
  • Fire detection, raising the alarm and safe evacuation procedures
  • Roles and responsibilities of the fire marshal in early years settings
  • Planning, conducting and reviewing fire drills

Practical information

  • What you need: Desktop, tablet or mobile and internet access.
  • Assessment: Multiple-choice final assessment; 70% pass required; certificate available immediately on passing.
  • Support: Accessibility adjustments and learner support available on request.
