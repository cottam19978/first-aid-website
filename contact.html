FAQ

How do I request a bespoke in-house quote?

Use the form and choose "Group booking / in‑house" and include your preferred dates, delegate numbers and venue postcode.

Can I buy bulk e‑learning licences?

Yes — select "E‑learning licences" in the enquiry type and tell us how many licences you need. We offer admin access and reporting for larger orders.

What accessibility adjustments are available?

We provide reasonable adjustments such as extended breaks, printed materials, assisted technologies and physical access support. Contact us to discuss specifics.
