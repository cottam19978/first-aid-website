Course Details

This theory-based half-day course gives employees the knowledge to identify manual handling risks, understand responsibilities under UK Health & Safety law and apply safer working practices to prevent injury.

Duration, Assessment & Requirements

  • Duration: Half Day
  • Certificate validity: 3 Years
  • Assessment: Multiple Choice Exam
  • Course requirements: No prior first aid knowledge needed; comfortable clothing recommended; a smartphone or tablet is required to scan QR codes for the test.

What you'll learn

Manual Handling Awareness
  • Identify common manual handling injuries
  • Understand relevant laws and regulations
  • Learn employer and employee responsibilities
Risk Assessment
  • Carry out simple manual handling risk assessments
  • Recognise workplace hazards and high-risk tasks
  • Apply control measures to reduce risk
Safe Handling Principles
  • Understand the musculoskeletal system
  • Learn principles of movement, balance and centre of gravity
  • Explore lever systems and safe manual handling techniques

Practical information

  • What to bring: ID (if required), comfortable clothing, any personal medication, smartphone or tablet for test scanning.
  • Accessibility: Free extra help and adjustments are available; contact us to discuss specific needs.
  • Certification: Certificate issued within 48 hours of successful completion.
