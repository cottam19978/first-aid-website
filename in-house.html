Why choose in‑house training?

We bring CPD-accredited first aid, mental health and fire safety courses to your workplace. In‑house delivery reduces downtime, lets us adapt content to your policies and risks, and is often the most cost-effective option for groups.

  • Flexible scheduling: day, evening or weekend sessions to suit your business.
  • Tailored content: risk-led scenarios and policies specific to your setting.
  • Competitive pricing: transparent group costs with clear delegate limits and options.
  • Complete delivery: qualified trainers, all equipment and certificates supplied.

Typical delegates: businesses, schools, care homes, nurseries, community groups and sports clubs.

How the process works

  1. Enquiry: tell us your course, delegate count and preferred dates.
  2. Tailored quote: we send availability, pricing and a sample delegate brief.
  3. Confirm: accept the quote and pay a deposit (terms shown in the quote).
  4. Delivery: trainer attends with equipment; post-course certificates and records provided.

Typical group size and facilities

We usually recommend up to 12 delegates per practical session to maintain high trainer-to-delegate ratios. A clear room with tables, chairs and basic AV (projector or large screen) is ideal.
