Course Details

The Level 2 Award in Basic Life Support & Safe Use of an AED is a practical, supportive course combining demonstrations, hands-on practice and realistic scenarios to reinforce learning. It focuses on recognising cardiac arrest, performing effective CPR and using an AED safely.

Duration, Assessment & Requirements

  • Duration: Half Day
  • Certificate validity: 1 Year
  • Assessment: Practical assessment on CPR and AED use and short multiple-choice knowledge check
  • Course requirements: No prior first aid knowledge needed; minimum age 14 years; physically able to carry out practical elements; comfortable clothing recommended; a smartphone or tablet is required to scan QR codes for the test.

What you'll learn

Introduction to BLS and AEDs
  • What is Basic Life Support?
  • What is an AED and how it works
  • Recognising heart attack versus cardiac arrest
  • Chain of Survival and key statistics
CPR Practical Skills
  • Adult CPR technique and compression-ventilation ratios
  • Managing resuscitation difficulties and safe teamworking
  • Using manikins for realistic practice
AED Use and Safety
  • Safe AED application and shock delivery
  • Paediatric pads and considerations
  • Integration of AED use into adult CPR
Scenario Practice
  • Short scenario-based drills to build decision-making and confidence
  • Recognising when to escalate and contacting emergency services

Practical information

  • What to bring: ID, comfortable clothing, any personal medication, smartphone or tablet for test scanning.
  • Accessibility: If you require assistance due to physical limitations, contact us and we’ll accommodate where possible.
  • Certification: Certificate issued within 48 hours of successful completion.
