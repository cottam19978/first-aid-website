Course Details

This two-day supervisory course equips line managers and wellbeing leads with the skills to recognise mental health issues, support colleagues, and foster a positive mental health culture at work.

Duration, Assessment & Requirements

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Certificate validity: 3 Years
  • Assessment: Written MCQs and verbal tasks
  • Course requirements: No prior first aid knowledge needed; comfortable clothing recommended; a smartphone or tablet is required to scan QR codes for the test.

What you'll learn

Understanding Mental Health
  • Recognise signs and symptoms of mental ill-health and stress
  • Understand complex mental health conditions
  • Overcome stigma and promote awareness
Practical Support Skills
  • Use a Mental Health Action Plan to support individuals in crisis
  • Active listening and supportive conversation techniques
  • Identify reasonable adjustments and workplace accommodations
Workplace Systems and Referral
  • Find and signpost to external professional help and guidance
  • Develop return-to-work and stay-at-work plans
  • Embed supportive policies and manage sensitive conversations

Practical information

  • What to bring: ID (if required), comfortable clothing, any personal medication, smartphone or tablet for test scanning.
  • Accessibility: Free extra help and adjustments are available; contact us to discuss specific needs.
  • Certification: Certificate issued within 48 hours of successful completion.
