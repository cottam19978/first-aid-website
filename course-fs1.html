Course Details

This half-day Fire Safety Awareness course gives delegates the knowledge to identify fire risks, understand prevention measures and respond appropriately in the event of an emergency, in line with UK fire safety guidance.

Duration, Assessment & Requirements

  • Duration: Half Day
  • Certificate validity: 3 Years
  • Assessment: Multiple Choice Exam
  • Course requirements: No prior first aid knowledge needed; comfortable clothing recommended; a smartphone or tablet is required to scan QR codes for the test.

What you'll learn

Fire Awareness & Prevention
  • Common causes of fires in the workplace
  • Cost and impact of workplace fires
  • Understanding the fire triangle and how fires spread
  • Key elements of fire risk management
  • Principles of fire prevention and control
Emergency Response
  • Actions to take in the event of a fire
  • Safe use of portable fire-fighting equipment
  • Safe evacuation procedures
  • Duties and responsibilities of all employees
Legal Responsibilities
  • Fire safety laws and regulations
  • Employer and employee responsibilities
  • Fire safety checks and basic inspections

Practical information

  • What to bring: ID (if required), comfortable clothing, any personal medication, smartphone or tablet for test scanning.
  • Accessibility: Free extra help and adjustments are available; contact us to discuss specific needs.
  • Certification: Certificate issued within 48 hours of successful completion.
