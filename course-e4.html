Course Details

This online First Aid for Parents course gives practical guidance for emergencies involving babies and young children. The course uses short videos, interactive content and scenario-based checks so you can learn at your own pace and build confidence to act when it matters.

Who is this course for

Ideal for parents, expectant parents, grandparents, babysitters, carers and anyone regularly caring for infants or young children. No prior first aid experience required.

Assessment and Certification

The course includes self-check activities and an optional multiple-choice quiz at the end. Successful learners receive a downloadable certificate to demonstrate completion.

Course Topics Include

  • Assessing an emergency and knowing what to do first
  • Helping an unresponsive child or baby (breathing and not breathing)
  • Seizures and febrile convulsions
  • Choking and hands-on choking response guidance
  • Severe bleeding and hypovolemic shock
  • Anaphylaxis and allergic reactions
  • Burns, scalds, fractures and head injuries
  • Meningitis signs and symptoms
  • Asthma attacks and inhaler guidance
  • Eye injuries and foreign objects
  • Bites, stings and common childhood conditions
  • What to include in a family first aid kit

Practical information

  • What you need: Desktop, tablet or mobile and internet access; smartphone or tablet for QR test if required.
  • Support: Accessibility help and additional learner support available on request.
  • Certificate: Downloadable certificate provided on successful completion; keep for records and childcare requirements.
