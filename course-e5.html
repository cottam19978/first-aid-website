Course Details

This 2-hour online course provides practical guidance for identifying, evaluating and managing risks in Early Years settings. Designed to help practitioners meet EYFS requirements and national health & safety regulations, it focuses on creating safer environments for children and staff.

Who is this course for

Suitable for nursery staff, childminders, preschool educators and teaching assistants working in Early Years settings. No prior qualifications required.

Assessment and Certification

Interactive lessons, quizzes and a final multiple-choice assessment (70% pass mark). Successful learners receive a downloadable CPD-accredited certificate and 2 CPD points.

What you'll learn

  • The importance of risk assessment and practitioner responsibilities
  • OFSTED inspection focus and recording for Early Years
  • Common risks in Early Years settings including slips, trips and falls
  • The 5 steps of risk assessment: identify hazards; decide who might be harmed; evaluate and control risks; record and action; review and update
  • How to produce simple, practical action plans and review cycles

Practical information

  • What you need: Desktop, tablet or mobile and internet access.
  • Assessment: Final multiple-choice assessment; 70% pass required; certificate available immediately on passing.
  • Support: Accessibility adjustments and learner support available on request.
