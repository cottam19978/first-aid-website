Course Details

This one-day Fire Marshal Training course equips delegates with the knowledge and practical skills to manage fire risks, conduct risk assessments, and carry out safe evacuations in line with UK fire safety legislation.

Duration, Assessment & Requirements

  • Duration: 6 Hours
  • Certificate validity: 3 Years
  • Assessment: Multiple Choice Exam
  • Course requirements: No prior first aid knowledge needed; comfortable clothing recommended; a smartphone or tablet is required to scan QR codes for the test.

What you'll learn

Fire Awareness & Prevention
  • Common causes of workplace fires
  • Fire triangle, fire behaviour and smoke spread
  • Fire hazards during and after an incident
  • Fire prevention and control measures
Emergency Procedures
  • Fire detection and raising the alarm
  • Safe evacuation and means of escape planning
  • Duties of the Fire Marshal during emergencies
  • Contents and delivery of a fire safety briefing
Fire Safety Laws & Responsibilities
  • UK Fire Safety Order and HSE requirements
  • Employer and employee responsibilities
  • How to perform fire risk assessments
  • Conducting fire safety inspections
Practical Fire Safety Skills
  • Using fire-fighting equipment safely
  • Understanding fire-fighting principles
  • Applying theory in real workplace scenarios

Practical information

  • What to bring: ID (if required), comfortable clothing, any personal medication, smartphone or tablet for test scanning.
  • Accessibility: Free extra help and adjustments are available; contact us to discuss specific needs.
  • Certification: Certificate issued within 48 hours of successful completion.
