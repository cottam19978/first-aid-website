Course Details

The two Day First Aid at Work (FAW) Requalification course refreshes essential first aid skills for delegates whose three day FAW certificate is approaching expiry. Successful completion renews the Level 3 FAW qualification, ensuring first aiders remain competent and compliant across a range of workplace environments.

Duration, Assessment & Requirements

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Certificate validity: 3 Years
  • Assessment: Practical tasks + 2 multiple-choice tests
  • Course requirements: If you previously completed the Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW) course, you only need to re-attend EFAW for renewal. No prior first aid knowledge needed; minimum age 14 years; physically able to carry out practical elements; comfortable clothing recommended; a smartphone or tablet is required to scan QR codes for the test.

What you'll learn

Emergency Response
  • Managing unresponsive casualties
  • Assessing incidents and scene safety
  • Calling for emergency assistance and incident recording
CPR & AED
  • Performing CPR and safe AED use
  • Managing resuscitation difficulties
  • Decision making under pressure
Recovery Position
  • Placement and continuous monitoring of breathing casualties
  • Scenario-based recovery practice
Serious Medical Conditions
  • Heart attack and stroke recognition and response
  • Epilepsy, asthma and diabetes management
  • Anaphylaxis and shock treatment
Injuries & Trauma Management
  • Head, spinal and chest injuries
  • Fractures, sprains and dislocations
  • Wounds, bleeding, burns, eye injuries and poisoning

Practical information

  • What to bring: ID, comfortable clothing, lunch for full days, any personal medication, smartphone or tablet for test scanning.
  • Accessibility: If you require assistance due to physical limitations, contact us and we’ll accommodate where possible.
  • Certification: Certificate issued within 48 hours of successful completion.
