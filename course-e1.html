Course Details

This 2-hour online Annual First Aid Refresher is designed to help first aiders refresh their knowledge and skills between full qualifications. It includes short videos, clear audio explanations, interactive quizzes and practical scenario guidance, finishing with a short multiple-choice assessment.

Who is this course for

Suitable for anyone who has completed First Aid at Work, Emergency First Aid at Work or the FAW Requalification and wishes to refresh skills during the three-year certification period. Also useful for individuals interested in revisiting essential first aid principles.

What to Expect

  • Short videos and clear audio explanations
  • Interactive quizzes and knowledge checks
  • Practical scenarios with guidance for real-world application
  • Short multiple-choice assessment at the end

Course Content

  • Roles and responsibilities of a First Aider
  • How to respond to an unconscious casualty
  • Seizures and recovery position
  • CPR (including COVID-19 adaptations)
  • Using an AED (defibrillator)
  • Choking
  • External bleeding and shock
  • Burns and minor injuries

Practical information

  • What you need: Desktop, tablet or mobile; internet connection; smartphone or tablet for QR test if required.
  • Assessment: Short multiple-choice test; certificate available to download immediately on successful completion.
  • Support: Accessibility help and additional learner support available on request.
